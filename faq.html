FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out anytime — we're glad to answer questions before you commit to anything.

The Starter Care plan includes basic website monitoring, basic backups, email support, minor technical fixes, and one very small content update every three months. It's designed for websites that mainly need to stay online, secure, and healthy.
Hosting details are confirmed as part of your website agreement. In many cases hosting is arranged on your behalf and included as part of your plan; in others it may be billed separately. We'll always be clear about which applies to your project before you commit.
Cancellation terms depend on the care plan and client agreement you've signed. Details are outlined in your Website Care Plan Terms, which we're happy to walk through with you before you sign up.
Small updates include things like changing your business hours, updating a phone number or email address, correcting text, replacing one image, updating a price, adding a short announcement, or fixing a minor website issue.
Large changes include adding new pages, redesigning sections, adding booking or payment systems, creating an online store, adding custom forms, adding memberships or login systems, writing large amounts of new content, major SEO work, or a full website redesign. These are quoted and approved separately from your care plan.
Website development starts at $399 for a Starter Website, $699 for a Business Website, and Advanced Websites (with features like online stores or booking systems) are custom-quoted. Final pricing depends on pages, features, content, and complexity.
